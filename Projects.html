Fundraising event for a mother with cancer

Three years ago , A mother of a very good friend of mine got cancer .
Treating the disease requires a lot of money, so me and other friends of mine organized a fundraising event for her.
First, we looked for a large place that could host us voluntarily and people who would come to help arrange everything.
Within a few days we managed to get a lot of donors and volunteers and we managed to raise a considerable amount of money, we even managed to get several singers to come voluntarily to get more people to come.


In addition, we recruited a graphic designer who designed the flyer that we distributed in localities near the event location.
The flyer is attached to the image on this page.

This was very satisfying and a significant moment in my life.
